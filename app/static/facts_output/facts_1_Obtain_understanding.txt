Batch 1:
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Compass Group uses several significant computer applications for various transaction classes. Agilysys is used for point of sale restaurant transactions and payments. It is provided by Agilysys as a service organization, is not internally developed, has no custom features, and Compass Group does not have access to its source code. The company's dining website is built on Squarespace, involves no transactions, is internally developed by Bon Appetit, and Compass Group has access to its source code. Triple Seat is used for event management without payment processing, is provided by TripleSeat as a service organization, has no custom features, and Compass Group does not have access to its source code. Authorize.Net is used for event credit card payments, is provided by Authorize.Net as a service organization, has no custom features, and Compass Group does not have access to its source code.
